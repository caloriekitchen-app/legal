8. AI Premium Features

Silver and Gold subscriptions include AI photo scanning (meal photos and fridge photos). Bronze and Gold subscriptions include AI data insights (recipe suggestions from your fridge, and a weekly progress observation). Using these features means:

  • A photo you take of a meal or your fridge is sent to Google's Gemini API to identify the food or ingredients shown. We do not store these photos — they are processed and then discarded.
  • For meal photo scanning, identified food names are also sent to the USDA FoodData Central public database (a US government nutrition database) to look up calorie and macro information. No personal data is sent to USDA — only a food name and an estimated portion size.
  • For AI data insights, a compact summary of your own data (e.g. recent food names, daily calorie/protein totals, dietary preference, fridge contents) — never your full diary history or raw entries — is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate suggestions or a short observation.
  • None of these features use free-form chat: you never type a prompt to the AI, and every response is a short, structured suggestion or observation, not a conversation.

These features are opt-in by nature — they only activate when you purchase the relevant subscription tier and actively use them (taking a photo, tapping a suggestion button).
